Specifications

Card stock

400gsm premium cardstock (standard). 350gsm and 450gsm options available for different budgets and premium feel.

Finish options

Matte finish (standard), glossy lamination, spot UV coating, foil stamping (gold/silver), and embossing available.

Print quality

Full-color CMYK printing with 300 DPI resolution. Pantone spot colors available for brand matching.

File requirements

PDF, AI, or PSD files. 3mm bleed required (3.75" x 2.25"). Minimum 300 DPI resolution. CMYK color mode.

Frequently asked

Can you help design the business cards?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing layout. Design starts from ₦15,000.

What's the standard business card size?

Standard size is 3.5" x 2" (89 x 51mm). We can also print custom sizes upon request.

Do you print variable data?

We support unique names, titles, or contact information. Send an Excel sheet and we will personalise each card at scale.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What corner styles are available?

Square corners (standard) or rounded corners (3mm radius). Custom corner shapes available for premium orders.
