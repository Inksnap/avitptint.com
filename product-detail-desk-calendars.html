Frequently asked

Can you help design the calendar?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing layout. Design starts from ₦15,000.

Can I customize each monthly page?

Yes. Each monthly page can have custom images, branding, or special date highlights. Standard calendar grid included.

What's included with the calendar?

Each calendar includes 12 monthly pages, cover page, and a desk stand for display.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

Minimum order is 50 calendars. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
