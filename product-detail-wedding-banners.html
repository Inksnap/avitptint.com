Frequently asked

Can you help design the banners?

Yes. Our in-house designers specialize in wedding designs. Design service starts from ₦30,000.

Can I use it outdoors?

Yes. Our banners are weather-resistant and suitable for outdoor use. UV protection ensures colors don't fade.

How do I install the banner?

Banners can be hung using grommets, velcro, or stand systems. We can provide installation guidance or coordinate installation services.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate delivery to wedding venues.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

How long does the banner last?

With proper care, banners last 2-3 years for indoor use and 1-2 years for outdoor use depending on exposure.
