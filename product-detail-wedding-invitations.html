Frequently asked

Can you help design the invitations?

Yes. Our in-house designers specialize in wedding designs. Design service starts from ₦20,000.

What's included with the invitations?

Each set includes the main invitation card and RSVP card. Envelopes and additional inserts available.

Can I customize the text?

Yes. We can customize all text including names, dates, venues, and special messages.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate delivery to venues.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs and physical samples for approval before final production.

Minimum order is 50 invitations. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
