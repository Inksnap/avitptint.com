Frequently asked

Can you help design the brochures?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing layout. Design starts from ₦15,000.

What's the difference between tri-fold and bi-fold?

Tri-fold has 3 panels (6 pages), bi-fold has 2 panels (4 pages). Tri-fold offers more space for detailed information.

Can I print on both sides?

Yes. Both sides are printed as standard. Single-sided printing available upon request.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 100 brochures. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
