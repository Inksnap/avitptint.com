Our mission

Avitprint exists to remove friction from how African teams create, manage and distribute printed assets. We believe access to world-class production should be as fast as sending an email-without sacrificing craft or accountability.

Craft first

Our production partners are vetted for colour accuracy, eco-friendly inputs and ethical labour practices.

Human support

Every account gets a dedicated success specialist who understands your brand and manages every detail.

Intelligent logistics

We blend digital approvals, smart routing and real-time tracking so you can deploy campaigns with confidence.

A timeline of steady growth

  1. 2014

    Launched Avitprint with a single production line in Lagos, focused on business cards and flyers for SMEs.

  2. 2017

    Opened our first fulfilment hub and introduced digital storefronts so teams could reorder assets on demand.

  3. 2020

    Expanded to Abuja and Port Harcourt, rolling out nationwide courier partnerships for 72-hour delivery.

  4. 2023

    Launched Avitprint Cloud-an asset management layer that lets distributed teams launch campaigns in one click.

  5. Today

    Powering product launches, retail rollouts and HR experiences for fast-scaling teams across West Africa.
