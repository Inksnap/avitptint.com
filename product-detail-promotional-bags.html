Frequently asked

Can you help design the bags?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing layout. Design starts from ₦15,000.

What's the difference between tote and jute bags?

Tote bags are typically canvas/cotton, more durable. Jute bags are eco-friendly and biodegradable, perfect for sustainability campaigns.

How long does the print last?

Our printing is designed to last through regular use and washing. Print remains vibrant for the life of the bag.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 50 bags. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
