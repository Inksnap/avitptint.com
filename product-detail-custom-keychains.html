Frequently asked

Can you help design the keychains?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing logo. Design starts from ₦10,000.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 50 keychains. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.

How long does the print last?

Our printing and engraving are designed to last the life of the keychain. Print remains clear through regular use.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What shapes are available?

Standard rectangular, circular, or custom die-cut shapes available. Contact us for specific shape requirements.
