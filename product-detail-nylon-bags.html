Frequently asked

Yes. LD nylon material is fully waterproof, making these bags perfect for all weather conditions.

Minimum order is 100 bags. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.

How long does the print last?

Our spot color printing is designed to last the life of the bag. Print remains vibrant through regular use.

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.
