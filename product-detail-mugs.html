Frequently asked

Are the mugs dishwasher safe?

Yes. Our mugs are fully dishwasher and microwave safe. The print is permanent and won't fade with regular washing.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 25 mugs. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.

Can I print on both sides?

Yes. We can print on one or both sides of the mug. Full-wrap printing is also available.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What mug colors are available?

Standard white mugs are most common. Colored mugs available for bulk orders. Contact us for color options.
