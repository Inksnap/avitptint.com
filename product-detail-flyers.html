Frequently asked

Can you help design the flyers?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing layout. Design starts from ₦15,000.

What's the difference between A5 and A4?

A5 (148 x 210mm) is half the size of A4 (210 x 297mm). A5 is perfect for handouts and inserts, while A4 offers more space for detailed information.

Do you print variable data?

We support unique codes, names, or addresses. Send an Excel sheet and we will personalise each flyer at scale.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What about weather resistance?

Standard flyers are for indoor use. Laminated flyers withstand light moisture. For outdoor use, we recommend our waterproof PVC option.
