Frequently asked

Can you help design the covers?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing layout. Design starts from ₦15,000.

What's the difference between spiral and hard cover?

Spiral binding allows the jotter to lay flat when open. Hard cover provides more durability and a premium feel.

Can I customize the page layout?

Yes. We can add custom headers, footers, or page designs. Standard ruled, blank, or grid pages available.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate bulk distribution services.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 50 jotters. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
