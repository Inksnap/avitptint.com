Frequently asked

Can you help design the program?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create respectful designs from brief. Design service starts from ₦20,000.

What information should be included?

Typically includes photos, biographical information, service details, and memorial messages. We can customize based on your needs.

Can I include photos?

Yes. We can include multiple photos in the program. High-resolution photos required for best results.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate delivery to venues.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 50 programs. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
