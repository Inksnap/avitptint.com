Frequently asked

Can you help design the premium cards?

Yes. Our in-house designers specialize in luxury designs. Premium design service starts from ₦25,000.

What's the difference between spot UV and foil?

Spot UV creates a glossy raised effect. Foil stamping adds metallic gold or silver finish. Both can be combined for maximum impact.

Can I combine multiple finishes?

Yes. We can combine spot UV, foil, and embossing for the ultimate premium look. Contact us for combination pricing.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. Premium packaging included for luxury cards.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs and physical samples for premium orders before final production.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 100 cards. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
