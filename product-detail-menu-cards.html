Frequently asked

Can you help design the menus?

Yes. Our in-house designers can create concepts from brief or polish your existing layout. Design starts from ₦20,000.

Is lamination included?

Yes. Standard lamination is included to protect menus from spills and wear. Glossy or matte finish available.

Can I update the menu later?

Yes. We can print updated menus whenever you need changes. Reorder discounts available for existing customers.

Where do you deliver?

Nationwide delivery via courier partners. Lagos pickup is free. We can also coordinate delivery to venues.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes. We provide digital proofs for approval before production. Physical samples available for large orders.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 50 menus. For smaller quantities, please contact us for a custom quote.
